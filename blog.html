Disney World, Florida


Disney World is the most magical place on earth. Each park offers a variety of different rides, characters, shows, food, and environments, Hollywood Studios being my personal favorite. It is suitable for all ages and is a great place to go to to feel like a child again. From shows, rides, scavenger hunts, food and wine, art, parades, fireworks, light shows, and meeting characters, among other things, there is something enjoyable for everyone. If you are planning on visiting, hit me up and I'll go with you!


by Mayte Ramirez-Calderon



White Mountains, NH


Explore the wonderful mountains engulf both New Hampshire and Maine! Connecting Boston, New York City, Montreal and Quebec, these dazzling mountains are the perfect spot for anyone that wants to experience the beauty of the northeast! Especially during the autumn, the forests will leave your eyes in awe at the abundance of colors and hue caused by the sunrise or sunset.



NC Outer Banks


A 100-mile stretch of four barrier islands, the Outer Banks of North Carolina offer an alluring experience of the rhythms of nature and sea that flow through the scenic lighthouses, quaint villages, wild horses, windswept beaches, and scruffy crab shacks of the coast. Highway 12 connects much of the Outer Banks as well as its 400 years of history as commemorated in places like the Wright Brothers National Memorial and Kitty Hawk and Kill Devil Hills.


By Yunah Kim

Waterwheel, Payson, AZ


Waterwheel located in Payson, AZ is a wonderful get away full of scenic views. Not only is it a great place to cool off from the Arizona sun, but it is also a great getaway with the family. The trail includes a lot of hiking and allows you to be one with nature. Being in bear country you will be exposed to all sorts of animals including fish, birds, etc.
