NC Outer Banks


A 100-mile stretch of four barrier islands, the Outer Banks of North Carolina offer an alluring experience of the rhythms of nature and sea that flow through the scenic lighthouses, quaint villages, wild horses, windswept beaches, and scruffy crab shacks of the coast. Highway 12 connects much of the Outer Banks as well as its 400 years of history as commemorated in places like the Wright Brothers National Memorial and Kitty Hawk and Kill Devil Hills.


By Yunah Kim

Waterwheel, Payson, AZ


Waterwheel located in Payson, AZ is a wonderful get away full of scenic views. Not only is it a great place to cool off from the Arizona sun, but it is also a great getaway with the family. The trail includes a lot of hiking and allows you to be one with nature. Being in bear country you will be exposed to all sorts of animals including fish, birds, etc.
